CONDITIONS OF PARTICIPATION IN THE CONFERENCE AND PUBLISHING IN THE COLLECTION OF REPORTS

To participate in the conference, it is necessary to submit the following materials to the secretariat of the organizing committee by March 11, 2019: registration form; thesis of the report; a receipt for the payment of a registration fee in the amount of 200 UAH.

Students of the Taras Shevchenko National University are typed in a collection of abstracts for free.

Materials submitted without completing the application will not be considered.

REQUIREMENTS TO THE STUDY OF THE REPORT

No more than three collaborators per report are allowed.. Abstracts should convey the main content of the report and contain: the statement of the scientific problem (task) in general form; formulation of the task (purpose) of the research, substantiation of its relevance and information about used literature. The author is responsible for the scientific content and presentation of the material. Do not set page numbering. Abstracts of the report - up to 2-4 pages of full text.

Polygraph Requirements: page format – A5, Font - Times New Roman 11; line spacing - single; The indent of the first line of each paragraph is 0.5 cm.

Fields: top, left and right - 2.0; lower - 2,2. Formulas need to be embedded in text from the formula editor MathType (Microsoft Eq. 3.0).

Font of Times New Roman 9, bold.

When drawing up the list of literature it is necessary to apply a national standard, identical to GOST 7.1.-2003

Materials are typed in the following order:

  1. UDC - alignment on the left edge;
  2. initials and surnames of the authors - alignment on the right edge, bold font;
  3. the name of the organization, e-mail - alignment on the right-hand side, italics;
  4. title of the report - center alignment;
  5. the text of the report;
  6. Table headers - Right-aligned alignment.
