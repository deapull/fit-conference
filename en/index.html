ІI INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC AND PRACTICAL CONFERENCE “PROBLEMS of CYBERSECURITY OF INFORMATION AND TELECOMUNICATION SYSTEMS” (PCSIТS)

11-12 April 2019

General Information

Dear colleagues, friends!

Applications for participation in the 2nd INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC-PRACTICAL CONFERENCE "PROBLEMS OF CYBER SECURITY OF INFORMATION AND TELECOMMUNICATION SYSTEMS" (PCSI&ТS) are continuing, which will be held on April 11-12, 2019 at the address: 24 Bohdan Hawrylyshyn Str., Kyiv, Ukraine, 04116, phone (044) 481-44-78.

Important Dates

March 11 is the deadline for registration and submission of abstracts.

Until March 15 - transfer of the organizational fee.

11 April, 2019:
- from 09:00 to 10:00 – registration of conference participants (4th floor).
- from 09:00 to 16:00 – plenary meeting (Assembly Hall).
- from 12:00 to 13:00 – a break in the meeting.
- from 16:30 – friendly dinner.

12 April, 2019:
- from 10:00 to 16:00 the continuation of the Conference. Sectional meetings (see your audience number in the Conference program).
- from 14:30 – conclusions of the conference (Assembly Hall)

For more information, see the link below: Site of the Department of Cyber security and Information Protection of Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv – http://kbzi.knu.ua Phone: (044) 481-44-78.
